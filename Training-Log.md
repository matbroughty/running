**RPE scale** (Borg-style, simplified):
- 1–3: very easy, recovery
- 4–5: easy, conversational
- 6: steady
- 7: tempo / threshold
- 8: hard (10K race pace)
- 9: very hard (5K pace, intervals)
- 10: all-out
